A little about me:


I am a Senior at UCSD majoring in Cognitive Science, HCI. In this program, I have been enrolled in various courses in which I have designed various things, some of which you can view here on this site.

Opera Mea:

Latin for "My Works", Opera Mea is a showcase website for some of my artistic/design endeavors, as well as present an arena in which people can get into contact with me for help with Web Design, anything outdoors-advanture related, and even language tutoring in Spanish, Russian, and German.


Backcountry Photography:

Wyoming's grand, vast landscape makes it quite the 'prime-nugget' location, and consequently, it has always been among the top of my favorite palces to head into the backcountry and capture with my camera.
